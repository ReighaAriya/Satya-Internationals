What is Satya International School?

Satya International School is an educational institution founded in 2008 by Satya Internationals Co. in Indonesia. The school offers a globally focused curriculum aimed at providing high-quality education with an emphasis on international standards. Its mission is to cultivate well-rounded students who excel academically and culturally in a globalized world.

With a strong focus on academic excellence, the school incorporates international teaching standards while emphasizing critical thinking, creativity, and cross-cultural understanding. Satya International School aims to nurture well-rounded individuals who are not only equipped with knowledge but also with the skills and values necessary to thrive in both local and international settings.


The school is dedicated to fostering a supportive learning environment where students can excel in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities. By promoting both personal and intellectual growth, Satya International School strives to develop future leaders who are capable of making meaningful contributions to society on a global scale.
