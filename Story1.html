From Satya to Silicon Valley

Muhammad Farand Maulana Akbar's journey from Satya International School to becoming a successful entrepreneur in Silicon Valley.

Muhammad Farand Maulana Akbar, a Class of 2010 alumnus of Satya International School, has made a name for himself in the tech industry. After completing his studies at Satya, he went on to pursue his passion for technology and innovation. He started his career as a software engineer at a top tech firm in Silicon Valley, where he worked on several high-profile projects. His hard work and dedication quickly paid off, and he was soon promoted to a leadership role. Today, Muhammad is the CEO of his own tech startup, which has gained significant recognition in the industry. He credits his success to the strong foundation he built during his time at Satya International School. Farand’s journey didn’t stop there. As CEO of his tech startup, he led the company to develop groundbreaking solutions in artificial intelligence and machine learning. His startup quickly became known for its innovative products, attracting attention from major investors and tech giants.


Under Farand’s leadership, the company expanded its operations globally, opening offices in several countries. His vision and strategic thinking helped the startup secure numerous awards and accolades, further solidifying its reputation in the tech industry. Farand also became a sought-after speaker at international tech conferences, where he shared his insights and experiences with aspiring entrepreneurs. He remained committed to giving back to the community, mentoring young

tech enthusiasts and supporting educational initiatives. Despite his busy schedule, Farand always made time for his family and friends. He often credited his success to the support and encouragement he received from his loved ones. His story continues to inspire many, proving that with passion, dedication, and a strong foundation, anything is possible.
